Our Mission
Pioneering Clean Mobility
Founded in 2009, Team Hammerhead GIKI is Pakistan's longest-running and most decorated student EV design team. Operating out of the GIK Institute, we bring together students from various engineering disciplines to solve the real-world challenges of sustainable transportation.

Our work revolves around the design, simulation, fabrication, and testing of ultra-energy-efficient prototype vehicles. We compete internationally in the Shell Eco-Marathon, pushing technical limits to squeeze maximum distance from minimal electrical energy.

Zero-Emission Engineering
Developing battery-electric systems to accelerate the global transition to green energy.

Student Innovation Lab
Applying classroom theory to solve aerodynamics, composite fabrication, and power electronics challenges.

Engineering Pillars
Our Core Competencies
Composite Chassis
Aerodynamic carbon-fiber shells and ultra-lightweight space frames engineered in SOLIDWORKS and simulated in ANSYS for minimum drag.

Power Electronics
Custom MOSFET driver boards, regenerative braking circuitry, and optimized throttle control systems to minimize transmission power loss.

Smart Battery & BMS
Smart Lithium-Ion cells management with active temperature sensors, over-current cutoff relays, and live cell balancing protocols.

Telemetry & Strategy
IoT nodes transferring sensor data via CAN bus and LoRa wireless to pit lane monitors, optimizing real-time fuel-to-range driving strategy.
